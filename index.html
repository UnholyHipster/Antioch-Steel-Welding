What We Do

Precision Work.
Every Single Job.

From custom fabrication to complete trailer rebuilds, Richard and his team bring craftsmanship and care to every project.

Custom Welding & Fabrication

Structural welds, custom brackets, metal framing, and one-off fabrication projects built to your exact specs. MIG, TIG, and stick — Richard does it right the first time.

Trailer Rebuild & Repair

Utility trailers, toy haulers, and equipment trailers fully rebuilt from the frame up. Like the Google reviews say — "practically brand new." Structural repairs, decking, and welded reinforcements.

Steel Structure Fabrication

Gates, fencing, carports, and agricultural steel structures fabricated and installed. Built for the Florida climate — hot-dip galvanized or powder-coated on request.

Equipment Repair & Restoration

Cracked frames, broken hitches, worn attachments — if it's made of metal, Richard can repair it. Farm equipment, construction attachments, and commercial machinery all welcome.

Commercial Contractor Work

Antioch Steel works with property managers, contractors, and businesses. W-9 and certificate of insurance available on request. Quotes turned around the same day.

On-Site & Mobile Welding

Can't bring it to us? We come to you. Mobile welding services available for larger equipment, farm structures, and on-location repair jobs throughout the Plant City area.
